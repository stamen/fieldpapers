A fieldset is a group of fields you can associate with an atlas. If you want to, say, record the locations of all the fire hydrants in your neighbourhood, you might create a fieldset with details like: hydrant_ID, latitude, longitude, hydrant_condition, or something like that. Then, you can describe any point in your atlas using your fieldset.
