Make - step 1 - set map/print area

Tweak the position of the red bounding box to set the basic area of your atlas. Then, you can jiggle the various page settings to organize your printed output. You can also associate a particular fieldset* with your atlas.

The next step is to refine each page, and basically preview how your atlas will look.

* A fieldset is basically a bunch of fields that describe a type of thing. For example, a crime fieldset might contain fields like typeofcrime, latitude, longitude, timeofday, recordedby.
